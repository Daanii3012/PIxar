LIFE AT PIXAR

Culture of originals

At Pixar, we're all about originality. Some of the world's greatest thinkers, dreamers, and doers make up our vibrant studio community where everyone contributes.

INCLUSION & DIVERSITY

Where everyone belongs

Creativity shines brightest when it comes from diverse minds. At Pixar, we're committed to fostering a welcoming, respectful environment where you can be your authentic self.

And different is better

What makes us different brings us together. We continue to build teams that reflect the world. We're constantly seeking to increase representation, access to opportunity, and create a space where our unique identities and ideas are celebrated

"Our studio is a reflection of our commitment to an inclusive culture and community. By embracing diversity and celebrating our differences, we foster a thriving and exceptional studio that creates a sense of belonging and welcomes everyone."

REEMA BATNAGAR

VICE PRESIDENT, PEOPLE

OUR VALUES

Our values light the way

While each of us offers something different, our shared values guide our actions. They ground our work and can be seen and felt every day.

Community

Caring for each other.

Innovation

Redefining what's possible.

Ownership

When we all contribute, we all succeed.

Authenticity

Dig deep, be unique.

At Pixar, we ask our directors to make movies inspired by the things that fascinate them or bring them joy. Out of this, we trust that personal connections will emerge, which will in turn connect with our audiences. For a story to resonate universally, it must start by being truthful and emotional for those of us who create it. Our goal is to make movies that surprise, delight, and reveal something about what it means to be human.

PETE DOCTER

CHIEF CREATIVE OFFICER
