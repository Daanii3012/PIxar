INTERNSHIPS

Learn by doing

We offer a range of exciting and immersive internship opportunities. Participants can choose from specialized tracks—by learning through direct mentorship and hands-on training, you'll gain the experience of a lifetime

Production Internships

Deepen your skills as you learn about Pixar's unique filmmaking process. During this 12-week paid experience, you'll be immersed in the creative filmmaking process on a daily basis, collaborating with art and technology teams, completing hands- on projects, and attending special studio events.

Work directly with production teams on active film projects. Experience the day-to-day operations of bringing animated stories to life, from initial concept to final render. Collaborate with directors, artists, and technical staff to understand the complete filmmaking pipeline.

Participate in structured educational programs designed to build core animation and storytelling skills. Attend workshops, masterclasses, and mentorship sessions that cover everything from traditional drawing techniques to advanced digital animation methods.

Technology Internships

Join our technology teams and work on cutting-edge tools and systems that power Pixar's creative process. You'll collaborate with engineers and artists to develop innovative solutions that push the boundaries of animation and storytelling technology.

Build next-generation software tools used by Pixar artists worldwide. Work on rendering engines, animation systems, and custom applications. Gain hands-on experience with C++, Python, and advanced graphics programming while solving real production challenges.

Optimize the technical workflows that enable seamless film production. Focus on pipeline automation, data management, and system integration. Learn about render farm operations, asset tracking, and the infrastructure behind blockbuster animated films.

Studio Operations Internships

Experience the business side of filmmaking through our studio operations internships. Work alongside professionals in areas like marketing, finance, human resources, and business development to understand how creative vision becomes commercial success.

Drive strategic initiatives and partnerships that expand Pixar's reach. Analyze market trends, evaluate licensing opportunities, and support business planning. Work with cross-functional teams to identify growth opportunities and help shape the studio's future direction.

Craft compelling narratives that connect Pixar films with global audiences. Develop social media campaigns, create promotional content, and support public relations efforts. Learn the art of storytelling beyond the screen while building brand awareness and engagement.

Studio Residents

Over the course of a residency, you'll collaborate directly with Pixar employees on studio projects as an integral part of the team.

While working at the studio, you'll be part of Pixar's vibrant community. From screenings and lectures to social health and wellness, you'll gain access to a wide range of company events, initiatives, and amenities to learn and grow—personally and professionally.

Residencies are deeply immersive. Programs can last for up to one year. For those who are eligible, competitive compensation and relocation assistance are available.

Our Studio Resident program focuses on advanced research and development in key areas including computer graphics, machine learning applications in animation, procedural modeling, advanced rendering techniques, and innovative storytelling tools. Residents work on cutting-edge projects that push the boundaries of animation technology.
